Our Service

01

Disease Prediction

Our specialized module utilizes machine learning and advanced algorithms to diagnose canine illnesses by analyzing observable symptoms. It offers reliable diagnoses and treatment recommendations, enhancing outcomes for dogs and assisting veterinarians and owners in identifying potential health issues.

02

Vet Recomendation

Our module employs advanced location-based algorithms to locate the nearest veterinarians across cities and sectors. By analyzing real-time location data, it offers reliable information on nearby veterinary clinics, enabling pet owners to promptly find essential medical care for their dogs.

03

Breed Recommendation

Our module utilizes various AI algorithms to analyze user preferences, lifestyle factors, and dog breed characteristics. By considering these factors, it suggests the most suitable dog breed, helping individuals find the perfect companion based on their unique needs and preferences.

04

Breed Classification

Our module utilizes artificial intelligence to accurately identify the specific breed of a dog based on visual features. By analyzing patterns and characteristics, it provides detailed information about the breed, enabling users to gain precise knowledge about a dog's lineage and characteristics.
